Dhoni was selected in 1998 by Deval Sahay to play for the Central CoalFields Limited (CCL) team.

This was the first time that Dhoni stepped into professional cricket.

Dhoni showed exceptional talent which impressed Deval Sahay who used his contacts

in Bihar Cricket Association to push for his selection in the Bihar team. Dhoni debuted for the senior Bihar Ranji Team for the 1999–2000 season. He was also included in the Bihar U-19 squad for the 1998–99 season where he scored 176 runs in 7 innings from 5 matches.
